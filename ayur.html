Introduction

"Ayurveda is considered by many scholars to be the oldest healing science. In Sanskrit, Ayurveda means “The Science of Life.” Ayurvedic knowledge originated in India more than 5,000 years ago and is often called the “Mother of All Healing.”"
"According to Ayurvedic philosophy the entire cosmos is an interplay of the energies of the five great elements—Space, Air, Fire, Water and Earth. Vata, pitta and kapha are combinations and permutations of these five elements that manifest as patterns present in all creation. In the physical body, vata is the subtle energy of movement, pitta the energy of digestion and metabolism, and kapha the energy that forms the body’s structure."
This app to provide our users a self-healing tool.

Usage

Ayurveda Wellness is a “self-healing” tool. The application provides the users with four main tools to let them heal themselves and bring a positive change in themselves. The user can write what they are grateful for each day. They can take the quiz to determine their own mind-body type. Moreover, the users can practice yoga postures with a counter and listen to sounds according to their unique mind-body type.
